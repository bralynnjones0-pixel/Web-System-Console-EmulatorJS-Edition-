Network & Troubleshooting Guide:

  • PSP, PS1, and 3DS only work outside of school networks.
  • If games lag, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

Unzipping Files:

If you have a .zip or .7z file that won't open, unzip it at https://extract.me/ before loading.
